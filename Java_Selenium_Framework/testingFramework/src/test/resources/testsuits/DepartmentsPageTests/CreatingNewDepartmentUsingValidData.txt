Creating new company using the valid data.
Preconditions:
1. The user is logined into the site.
2. The main page of the site is opened.

1. Move the mouse over the dropdown panel on the left side of the screen 
2. Click on the "Departments" button in the Pop-up menu
3. Click on the "New Department" button
4. Fill in the "Title" field and leave all other fields empty, click on the "Submit" button
Expected result:
The new department is successfully created and added to the list of departments
