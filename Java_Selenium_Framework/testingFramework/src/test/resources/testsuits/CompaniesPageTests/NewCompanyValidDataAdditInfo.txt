ID: 2
Summary:
Creating new company and filling additional information exept email, using the valid data 
Preconditions:
1. The user is logined into the site.
2. The main page of the site is opened.
Step actions:
1. Move the mouse over the dropdown panel on the left side of the screen 
2. Click on the "Companies" button in the Pop-up menu
3. Click on the "New Company" button
4. Fill in all the required fields and in the "Service List" table check "2 level" checkbox of the "Extended Service Program" 
5. Click on the "Additional information +" button and fill in all fields exept the "Email" field, click on the "Submit" button
Expected result:
The new company is successfully created and added to the list of companies
